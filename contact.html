Common Questions

Can I submit an article or tip? ▼

Absolutely! Use the contact form and select "Article Pitch / Tip" as the subject. We review all submissions and credit contributors.

Do you offer advertising opportunities? ▼

Yes, we offer display advertising and sponsored content opportunities for card-related businesses. Email press@actcollectibles.com for our media kit.

Can you value my card collection? ▼

We don't offer individual appraisal services, but our articles and price guides can help. For formal appraisals, we recommend PSA or Beckett's official services.
