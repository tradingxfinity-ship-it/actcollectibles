Our Story

Started at a Card Show in 2019

ACT Collectibles was born at a weekend card show in Chicago when five friends — all lifelong collectors — realized there was a gap in the market. Most card content online was either overly technical, focused only on mega-investors, or buried in YouTube rabbit holes.

We wanted a clean, well-written, trustworthy blog. One that explained PSA grading to beginners and also gave veteran collectors data-driven market analysis. One that covered Mickey Mantle T206s and 1st Edition Charizards with equal enthusiasm.

Six years later, ACT Collectibles has published over 500 articles, built a newsletter community of 120,000+ subscribers, and become one of the most-cited card collecting resources in the U.S. hobby space.

Our Values

The principles that guide every article we write

✅

Accuracy First

We verify prices, grades, and historical data before publishing. Our articles are fact-checked by multiple team members.

🔓

No Paywalls

All our guides and articles are free. We believe quality card collecting education should be accessible to everyone, from beginners to pros.

⚖️

Independent & Unbiased

We don't take sponsorships that influence our editorial coverage. Our recommendations are based on research, not paid promotions.

Community-Driven

We listen to our readers. Many of our best articles come from questions submitted by our community of collectors across the United States.
