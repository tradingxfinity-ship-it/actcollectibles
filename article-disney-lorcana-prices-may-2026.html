Disney Lorcana Enchanted Rares Are Climbing in May 2026 — Here's What's Moving and Why

SR
Sarah Rodriguez
TCG Market Editor · ACT Collectibles

Disney Lorcana's most premium cards are on the move in May 2026. TCGplayer's price trend report from May 10 confirmed consistent upward movement across Enchanted Rare variants — the rarest pull tier in the game at approximately one per two booster boxes. Elsa - Spirit of Winter from The First Chapter has touched $850, while Iconic Pocahontas from the Winterspell set approaches $200. Here's what's behind the surge and where the smart money is looking.

Why Enchanted Rares Are Appreciating

Enchanted Rares have a pull rate of roughly one per 48 packs — making them meaningfully scarce without being lottery-level rare. What drives their value beyond scarcity is the intersection of Disney character popularity and card aesthetics. Lorcana Enchanted Rares feature full-art, often borderless or landscape-format designs that display beautifully. They function as wall art as much as trading cards, which expands the buyer pool beyond competitive players to Disney collectors, parents buying for children, and adult fans of the properties.

The May 2026 price movement is partially supply-driven: older sets are increasingly scarce at retail, and new set releases are bringing new collectors into the game who then backfill earlier sets for key characters. Elsa, from Frozen, commands the highest premium in the game because Frozen remains Disney's most commercially potent animated franchise globally.

Elsa - Spirit of Winter (The First Chapter): $650–$850

Pull Rate: ~1 per 2 booster boxes (48 packs)

New Sets Driving Interest: Pixar Integration

The set called Wilds Unknown marks Pixar's full integration into Lorcana — featuring characters from Toy Story, The Incredibles, Monsters Inc., and Finding Nemo. For collectors who grew up with Pixar films, this is a significant emotional entry point. Woody, Buzz Lightyear, Elastigirl, and Mr. Incredible joining the Lorcana card pool means an entirely new wave of character-driven demand. Early previews for Wilds Unknown Enchanted Rares have already generated secondary market pre-order prices above $100 for the most anticipated designs.

The February 2026 set brought Pocahontas, Lilo and Stitch: The Series, and Darkwing Duck into the game for the first time. The Iconic Pocahontas Enchanted Rare shot up in value rapidly because of a combination of factors: beloved character with no prior Lorcana card, one-set scarcity (Iconic cards appear only in their debut set), and high aesthetic quality of the card art.

The 2026 Collector's Guide and Merchandise Expansion

Ravensburger launched official Disney Lorcana Collector's Guide books and expanded licensed merchandise in 2026, signaling the game's maturity as a mainstream hobby product beyond competitive play. Official collector's guides validate the investment conversation and bring in buyers who weren't previously engaged with the TCG market. For price trends, mainstream validation events like official collector's guides historically coincide with increased secondary market activity for key cards.

Where to Focus Your Lorcana Budget

For investment-minded collectors, the hierarchy is clear: First Chapter Enchanted Rares from Elsa and Mickey are the most liquid high-value assets. Second-set Enchanted Rares from Pixar integration sets (once released) will have the broadest appeal. Avoid early set reprints — Ravensburger has shown willingness to reprint sought-after cards in premium products, which creates a ceiling on price appreciation for non-Enchanted variants.

💡
May 2026 Lorcana Action Items: Monitor Elsa - Spirit of Winter for continued upward movement. Buy Iconic Pocahontas now before Wilds Unknown drives crossover attention to Winterspell. Watch for Pixar Enchanted Rare pre-sale listings — buying before full reveal keeps price exposure lower.
